# Demo Video Recording Script

Follow these steps exactly to record the final submission demo video. This flow ensures all core features are highlighted for the evaluator.

### Steps to Record
1. **App launch & Login screen**: Start the recording here. Wait 2 seconds to show the new dark theme, glow effects, and custom Google button.
2. **Tap 'Browse as Guest'**: (Or use the 'Continue with Google' / Email options if you prefer). This will trigger the auth flow and navigate to the Profile Setup.
3. **Profile setup**: Type your name (e.g., "Rahul Sharma") and optionally tap the avatar to pick a photo from your device. Tap "Continue".
4. **Home screen**: Wait for the grid to load. Slowly scroll through the grid to show the live overlays (your name and photo) and the Unsplash image backgrounds.
5. **Tap a category pill**: Tap "Birthday" or "Anniversary" to show the grid filtering dynamically.
6. **Tap a 🔒 Premium card**: Tap any card with the Premium badge. Show the elegant Premium subscription modal appearing.
7. **Dismiss modal**: Tap the "X" or "Maybe Later" to close the modal. Then, tap a free card.
8. **Full-screen card detail**: Wait for the card preview to render. Show that your name and photo overlay are perfectly visible on this screen as well.
9. **Tap Share**: Tap the large orange "Share & Spread" (or "Download Image" on web) button. Show the native share sheet or the download action succeeding.

Once complete, stop the recording and add the link to your `README.md`.
